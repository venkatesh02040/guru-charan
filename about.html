Profile

Meet the Doctor

Dr. Gurucharan S Shetty is a highly regarded Interventional Radiologist currently serving as Senior Consultant at Sparsh Hospitals, Sarjapur Road, Bengaluru. With extensive training from premier medical institutions across India and abroad, he has built a strong reputation in advanced image-guided interventions.

He has held key positions including Chief Interventional Radiologist at Sri Shankara Cancer Hospital and Consultant at Fortis Hospital, Bannerghatta Road, where he worked for nearly eight years. His clinical expertise spans diagnostic imaging as well as complex body and vascular interventional procedures.

Dr. Shetty has undergone advanced training at AIIMS New Delhi and completed specialised fellowships in neuroradiology and neurointervention at SGPGI, Lucknow, along with international exposure in Paris. He also holds prestigious European certifications, reflecting his commitment to excellence and high standards of patient care.

Philosophy

Why Interventional Radiology?

Interventional Radiology is transforming modern medicine by enabling highly targeted treatments through minimally invasive techniques. It allows complex conditions to be treated with greater accuracy while reducing the physical burden on patients.

These procedures are typically performed using small needle punctures under advanced imaging guidance, resulting in minimal pain, reduced hospital stay, quicker recovery, and significantly lower complication risks compared to traditional surgical methods.

"My approach is to provide patients with the safest and least invasive treatment options, using advanced imaging to ensure precision and better clinical outcomes."
