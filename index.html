About the Doctor

Dr. Gurucharan S Shetty

Dr. Gurucharan S Shetty is one of Bangalore’s most distinguished Interventional Radiologists, with over a decade of experience in performing complex, minimally invasive, image-guided procedures. He holds a PDCC in Neuroradiology and Interventional Radiology and is European Board certified in Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology (EDiR & EBIR).

Practicing at Sparsh Hospitals, Bengaluru, he is a pioneer in neuro-interventional procedures, vascular interventions, and oncology-related interventional radiology across South India.

Neuro-Interventional Expert: Mechanical Thrombectomy, Brain Aneurysm Coiling, AVM treatment

Oncology IR: TACE, Tumour Ablation, Preoperative Embolisation

Vascular Specialist: Varicose Veins, UAE, Angioplasty & Stenting

Critical Care IR: Advanced Stroke & Emergency Bleeding Interventions

After the scan was complete, Dr. Gurucharan S Shetty was called to look at the scan, he had a focused assessment about the problem. We were then told that there was an injury to the uterine artery, he further suggested a line of procedures to solve the issue. Dr. Gurucharan took it as a challenge to cure me – he was fighting battles on 2 fronts. First to convince my family and second to get ready for technical procedure since it was complicated. I really appreciate his knowledge, skill, enthusiasm and dedication. I can’t thank Dr. Gurucharan for the overall care & support that he provided.
